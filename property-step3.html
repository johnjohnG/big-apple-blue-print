Step 3: The Application Process

Submitting an Application

Because the market moves quickly, it is common to apply the same day you view a place. Standard requirements include:

  • ID: Government-issued passport.
  • Income: Recent pay stubs, an employment letter, or tax returns.
  • Financials: Recent bank statements and a credit check authorization.

Application Fees: In NYC, rental application fees are legally capped at $20 per applicant for background and credit checks.

STOP

Never wire large sums of money before viewing an apartment and reviewing a lease.

What if you have no US Credit Score?

Newcomers often face hurdles because they lack a U.S. credit history. Common workarounds include:

  • Higher Upfront Payments: Offering additional rent in advance (where legally permitted).
  • Third-Party Guarantors: Services like TheGuarantors or Insurent can act as a co-signer for a fee (usually around one month's rent). Note that these services will seek reimbursement from you if they have to pay the landlord.
