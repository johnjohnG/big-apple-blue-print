The Story Behind the Site

G'day! My name is Nurit and not too long ago, my husband and I made the 10,000-mile journey from Australia to the United States.

Moving to the U.S. is an incredible adventure, but it is also a logistical maze. From building a credit score from zero to navigating the unique world of New York rentals, the learning curve is steep. This website was created to be a guide of "good stuff to know" for those thinking about the move or those who have just arrived and are finding their footing in the Empire State.

Educational Purpose & Credibility

I am a qualified attorney who loves making the "fine print" easy to digest.

However, I am not your lawyer. This site is for educational purposes only and is not formal legal advice. It should not replace professional counsel regarding your specific circumstances. My goal is to empower you to navigate your new home with confidence.

What You'll Find Here

I've broken down the four essential pillars of establishing a life in the New York area:

  • Rental Properties: Navigating the real estate market and understanding tenant expectations.
  • Employment Rights: A guide to workplace protections and labor standards.
  • Immigration Roadmap: High-level overviews for staying organized during the process.
  • Banking & Credit: Practical steps for building a financial reputation from scratch.

Moving to New York is a bold step. I hope these resources make your transition smoother, clearer, and more enjoyable.
